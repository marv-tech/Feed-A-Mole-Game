Feed A Mole Game

How to play

  • Click on the 'Play Game' button to start
  • The worm meter indicates your game progress, feed enough moles to complete the worm meter
  • There are 4 mole states;
    • Hungry Mole: This indicates that the mole is hungry and you should click on it to feed it with a worm.
    • Fed Mole: This shows a mole with a worm in it's mouth indicating it has been fed.
    • Sad Mole: This indicates that the mole has not been fed.
    • Leaving Mole: This shows a mole leaving the hole.
  • There are king moles that could give you double points, aim for them.
  • Each click gives you a point, feed the mole quickly before it disappears.
